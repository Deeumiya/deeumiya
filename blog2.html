WHAT CAN GENE-EDITING DO

  • by:deeumiya
  • date:November 20/ 2019

To research the crop, we can not just stay in the field, we need to get into the molecular lever to explore more details. Besides, we also need to use the statistical software to help us doing the research.

In my lab, we use the "gene-editing" skill to create the crop's traits we want.

We are not the gods, but we are scientists.

In recent years, genetic modification is very controversial. Lots of people think that" We are not the gods, don't do the thing what gods are doing." They think that genetic modification make the creatures not natural. But I think that its correctness depands on the purpose and its outcome. For example, it we transplant a gene fragment to make the crop can resist pest. Then we can reduce the amount of pesticide we use.

Another problem that people concern is the foreign gene fragment that leave in crop will cause damage to human body. So our lab's professor and us use the gene-editing skill. Rather then other genetic modification methods, gene-editing doesn't need any foreign gene fragment. It can edits the creatures' gene on its own, just need a special protein called Cas9 and enzymes. And this skill is also cheaper and more efficient.

In our team, my job is to make the constra which will send into the plant through Agrobacteria. I learn a lot from the experiment process.
